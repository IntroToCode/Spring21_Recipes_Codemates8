# Scrambled eggs

## Servings: 4

## Time to prepare: 25 minutes

## Ingredients:
Eggs <br/>
Olive oil <br/>
Salt

## Equipment needed:
Spatula <br/>
Pan

## Steps for preparation:
Step 1: crack half a dozen eggs into a bowl <br/>
Step 2: add 2 tbsp olive oil to pan <br/>
Step 3: scramble eggs!
