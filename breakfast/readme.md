# Scrambled eggs

## Servings: 4

## Time to prepare: 25 minutes

## Ingredients:
Eggs
Olive oil

## Equipment needed:
Spatula
Pan

## Steps for preparation:
Step 1: crack half a dozen eggs into a bowl
Step 2: add 2 tbsp olive oil to pan
Step 3: scramble eggs!
